About Me

I am a student at Columbia Business School and the School of International & Public Affairs. My main areas of focus are risk, code, trust, data & Eurasia. Before school I was a cyber security consultant at Deloitte and since then I’ve been learning about business, policy, security, statistics and human behavior. I did my undergraduate at Washington & Lee University where I studied Economics and Russian Area Studies. I spend my free time on travel, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, reading and coding.
